What is SEO and explain the importance of SEO?

Ans-> SEO stands for Search Engine Optimization. It helps your websites so that they have either
rankings on Search Engines like Yahoo, Google and Bing as well.
The ultimate goal of SEO is to rank higher in search engine results pages for relevant keywords and phrases,
which can increase visibility, traffic and ultimately revenue for the websites.

The Importance of SEO :-> 
Meta tags are importance for SEO because they provide information to search engines about the content on yoursite
and can help improve your website's visibility and ranking in search engine results pages.
